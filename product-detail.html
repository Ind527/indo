Fresh Tomatoes Premium Grade Export Quality

Product Description

Our premium grade fresh tomatoes are carefully selected and harvested at peak ripeness to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional value. These tomatoes are perfect for export markets, offering exceptional quality, vibrant color, and extended shelf life.

Grown using sustainable farming practices in fertile soil with optimal climate conditions, our tomatoes meet international quality standards and food safety regulations. Each tomato is hand-picked and sorted to ensure only the finest quality reaches our customers.

Key Features:

  • Premium export quality grade A
  • Rich in vitamins C, K, and antioxidants
  • Extended shelf life of 2-3 weeks
  • Vibrant red color and firm texture
  • Pesticide-free and naturally grown
  • Available in various sizes (small, medium, large)
