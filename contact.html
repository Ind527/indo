Frequently Asked Questions

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product. Please contact our sales team for specific details based on your requirements.

Which countries do you export to?

We export to multiple countries across Asia, Europe, Americas, and Africa. Contact us to check if we serve your location.

How do you ensure product freshness?

We use state-of-the-art packaging and cold chain logistics to maintain optimal freshness throughout transportation.

What certifications do you have?

Our products come with international food safety certifications and comply with export regulations of destination countries.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on destination and shipping method. Typically, air freight takes 3-7 days, sea freight 15-30 days.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we provide product samples for serious buyers. Contact our sales team to discuss sample requirements and shipping.
